Bubbling and Capturing

Bubbling

When an event happens on an element, it first runs the handlers on it, then on its parent, then all the way up on other ancestors.
FORM - Clicking on me will only execute form
DIV - Clicking on me will execute alerts div > form

P - Clicking on me will execute all alerts from p > div > form

event.target

A handler on the parent element can identify where the event happened
